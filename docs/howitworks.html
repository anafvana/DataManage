How It Works

Below, you will be provided with some video tutorials on how to navigate your way through our app. Everything will be explained in english.


1. Getting ready with DataManage

In this video, we will demonstrate how to get started with DataManage. In the beginning, you will see how you can choose your preferred language. Then, you will be shown how to register with using minID. Once you get set, you can then always sign in using your fingerprint or personal code.


This is a link to the video transcription. The link will open in this browser tab you are currently in.

2. How to control my information

In this video, we will demonstrate how you can control your personal information in the app. Here, you can easily control which information that can be accessed online, and which information you want to prevent from being accessed online.


This is a link to the video transcription. The link will open in this browser tab you are currently in.

3. Personal search

In this video, you will get a list of every business that has some sort of information about you. You can filter by choosing to look specifically at online stores, sosial media or entertainment sites. When you get a list, you can click on who you wish to see what information they have stored about you, but also on what grounds. If you wish to gain access to the information they have about you, you will be able to request insight, correction and for your information to be deleted. The app will fix everything for you. A notification in the app will come when you get a reply on your request.


This is a link to the video transcription. The link will open in this browser tab you are currently in.

4. Business search

In this video, we will demonstrate how you can search for a specific business that has information stored about you in their databases. You will also be allowed to see on what grounds they collected that information. If you wish to gain access to the information they have about you, you will be able to request insight, correction and for your information to be deleted. The app will fix everything for you. A notification in the app will come when you get a reply on your request.
